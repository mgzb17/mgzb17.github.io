Happy Day! A moist northwest flow is generating mountain snow and light valley rain/snow showers across the region through this early afternoon. Snow showers will taper off this afternoon and skies will begin to clear across the region. In addition to the moisture, moderate to strong winds are expected across southern Idaho this afternoon. A Wind Advisory has been issued for the Upper Treasure Valley and the Magic Valley from 9 am to 5 pm today. Sustained winds of 25 to 35 mph are expected with gusts up to 40-45 mph. Wind will likely create difficult travel conditions throughout the day Friday. Over the weekend, northwest flow will remain in place. The Treasure Valley is expected to stay dry but will see partly cloudy skies through Sunday, temperatures in the mid to upper 40's for highs. Occasional snow showers are expected over the northern mountain regions as waves of moisture move within the northwest flow. Dry weather and cooler temperatures are currently in the forecast for next week. Stay tuned.
