Audience

The target audience of this app are those people wondering if their activity is safe to do depending on the weather. It is perfect for date activities, church activities, or family outings. Those people who loves to do outdoor activities will benefit a lot from this app. This app is designed to be simple to use so that even children as young as 8 or adults as old as 70 can navigate it.

Scenarios

1. A guy wanting to take a girl for a hike.
2. A church leader who is planning to go camping with the young men.
3. Parents who are deciding if they should go to the pool or the beach.
4. A counselor who is wanting to take a team to the ropes course for a team building activity.
5. A girl who is planning for a birthday party. Will the weather allow her to do it outdoors?.
6. A group of friends are wanting to hike to the Goldbug Hot Springs during the Winter but they are not sure if it will be snowing that day or not.
7. College students from Rexburg wants to go water tubing down the river. They want to make sure it will be warm enough to go.

Persona #1: Lexie Blaine

Lexie is a 23 year old college student who is studying Biology at the University of Washington. She is getting married in a week. The owners of the venue they rented for the reception is asking if they are supposed to decorate outside or inside. Washington is a state known for its rain. She was hoping to do the reception outside but she needs to know if it will be sunny or not. The RainShine Weather app can help her decide if it is still a good idea to celebrate her wedding outdoors. Through the app, she was able to tell the owner of the venue what her plans are.

Persona #2: Mario Cruz

Mario is the Young Men's President in his ward in Hawaii for the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints. He is 34 years old and loves to go camping. One Sunday, the boys were asking him that they want to go camping. He's been wanting to take them camping so he was really excited when they reached out. They live in the city of Kane'ohe which is in the windward side of the island of O'ahu. Contrary to people's belief that it is always summer in Hawaii, there is a reason why it is so green. That is because it rains a lot. The RainShine Weather app can help him decide when it is safe to go camping. It will give him an idea if the rain will be too much for the young men.
