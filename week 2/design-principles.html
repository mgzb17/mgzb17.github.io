Design Principles

by Madelle Bongalon

PROXIMITY

visualsociety.org

Proximity is when elements within the design are closer or farther depending on their relation to each other.

This is a good example of proximity because the information on the page are grouped well.

It is easy to tell which group of information are together.

ALIGNMENT

crunchyroll.com

Alignment, just like the word itself, is about how aligned the objects/texts are in a design.

The homepage of Crunchyroll did a good job because the texts are consistent with using center align.

The boxes of information are also aligned to each other.

VISUAL HIERARCHY

churchofjesuschrist.org

Visual Hierarchy is when the viewer can tell which elements of the design are more important than the others.

This website did a good job because it is easy for the viewer to tell which article is being suggested to be read first.

The eyes are guided where to look at first.
